How Short Sales Work

💰 Avoid Foreclosure

A short sale lets you sell your home for less than you owe, avoiding the credit damage and timeline of foreclosure.

📋 Relocation Assistance

Many lenders offer $3,000–$15,000 in relocation assistance to help you transition. Subject to lender approval.

⏱️ Faster Timeline

A short sale typically closes in 3–6 months, versus 6+ months for foreclosure. You stay in control of the process.

🛡️ Protect Your Credit

While a short sale does impact your credit, it's significantly less damaging than a foreclosure.

🤝 Professional Help
